Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student for success.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Toddler and Early Childhood programs provide a nurturing environment for young learners, fostering social, emotional, and cognitive development through hands-on activities and play-based learning.

📚

Elementary

In our Elementary program, students engage in a rigorous and engaging curriculum that emphasizes critical thinking, problem-solving, and collaboration. Our certified teachers provide individualized instruction and support to ensure each student achieves their full potential.

📊

Middle/Upper

Our Middle and Upper School programs offer a challenging and supportive environment for students to explore their interests and develop their passions. Our experienced teachers provide guidance and mentorship to help each student succeed and thrive.

💻

STEM

Our STEM program introduces students to the wonders of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics through hands-on projects and experiments. By emphasizing critical thinking and problem-solving, we prepare our students for a rapidly changing world.

🎨

Arts

Our Arts program offers a rich and diverse range of creative experiences, from music and dance to visual arts and drama. By fostering imagination and self-expression, we help our students develop their unique talents and interests.

🏈

Athletics

Our Athletics program provides a safe and supportive environment for students to engage in physical activity, develop teamwork and sportsmanship skills, and build lasting friendships.
