Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student for success.

🎒

Pre-K/Early Childhood

Our Pre-K program caters to 2- to 3-year-olds, fostering social, emotional, and cognitive growth. Our experienced teachers guide children through play-based activities and hands-on learning.

📚

Elementary

In our Elementary program, children from 6 to 9 years old embark on a journey of discovery, exploring a range of subjects, including language, math, science, and the arts.

📚

Middle/Upper

Our Middle and Upper programs cater to 10- to 12-year-olds, offering a challenging and engaging curriculum that prepares students for high school and beyond.

💻

STEM

Our STEM program focuses on science, technology, engineering, and math, providing students with hands-on experiences and project-based learning opportunities.

🎨

Arts

Our Arts program encourages creativity and self-expression, offering a range of activities, including music, dance, theater, and visual arts.

🏋️‍♀️

Athletics

Our Athletics program promotes physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship, offering various sports and activities for students of all ages and skill levels.
