From Our Head of School

As the Head of School at Austin Montessori, I am inspired by the resilience and curiosity of our students. Our school is a haven where children can grow, explore, and learn at their own pace, supported by dedicated teachers and a loving community.

Our Mission & History

Founded in 1997, Austin Montessori has been a beacon of Montessori education in the heart of Texas. Our school is dedicated to providing a balanced and holistic education that prepares children for a lifetime of learning.

Our teachers are trained in the authentic Montessori method, which emphasizes self-directed learning, hands-on activity, and collaborative play. We believe that every child is unique and deserves a tailored approach to education.

Beyond academics, we nurture a sense of community and character in our students. From art exhibits to science fairs, we encourage our students to explore their passions and develop essential life skills.
